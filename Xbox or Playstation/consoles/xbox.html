Xbox info

The Xbox is great because it offers powerful performance and smooth gameplay across all of its current-generation consoles. Its Game Pass subscription provides incredible value, giving players access to hundreds of games for one low monthly price. Xbox also excels in backward compatibility, allowing players to enjoy older games with improved graphics and performance.

Xbox Features:

  • Xbox Game Pass
  • Quick Resume
  • Strong backward compatibility
  • High performance
  • Affordable Series S model

Xbox Game Pass

Xbox offers three options for the game pass. The cheapest is the esssential, which is $9.99/month. The second option is premium which is $14.99/month. And the most expensive is $29.99/month

Game Pass video

Quick Resume

Xbox has a fantastic feature called Quick Resume, exclusive to the Xbox Series X and Series S, that lets you instantly jump between multiple games and pick up exactly where you left off, even after the console restarts or loses power, by saving game states to the SSD

Strong backward compatibility

Xbox has hundreds of backward-compatible games from the original Xbox and Xbox 360, totaling around 695 titles (63 original Xbox, 632 Xbox 360) as of the last major update in late 2021
